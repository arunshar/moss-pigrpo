MOSS @ COLM 2026 submission bundle
==================================

Venue:    Methods and Opportunities at Small Scale (MOSS), COLM 2026
Portal:   https://openreview.net/group?id=colmweb.org/COLM/2026/Workshop/MOSS
Deadline: June 30, 2026 (AoE)
Track:    Small-Scale Frontier (4-page main body)

WHAT TO UPLOAD TO OPENREVIEW (folder: UPLOAD_TO_OPENREVIEW/)
-----------------------------------------------------------
1. paper.pdf       -> the main submission PDF (4 pages, anonymized).
2. supplement.zip  -> supplementary artifact (the runnable notebook + code).

That is all that goes to OpenReview. Both files are anonymized and verified clean.

YOUR REFERENCE KIT (folder: reference/) -- do NOT upload these
--------------------------------------------------------------
- OPENREVIEW_FORM.md      -> paste-ready Title, Abstract, Keywords, TL;DR, FLOP statement.
- SUBMISSION_CHECKLIST.md -> full pre-submit checklist and double-blind rules.
- abstract.txt            -> the abstract as plain text.
- ANON_LINK.txt           -> optional: how to make a live anonymous.4open.science link.

REMINDERS
---------
- Enter your real name/email/affiliation in the OpenReview form fields only
  (OpenReview hides them from reviewers). Never in the PDF or notebook.
- Use your institutional (umn.edu) email for the OpenReview profile.
- Do not paste any github.com/arunshar/... URL in the submission.
